Welcome

Joje was founded in 1984 and We are a elegantly located beach from restaurant in Santa Barbara, California. Our goal is to offer High end fresh Japanese style Seafood. Enjoy a selection of appetizers, Sushi, fresh seafood, steak, salads, and hand crafted desserts including everything from classic favorites to modern cuisine, and one of the best drink menus in the world. Come join us for lunch or dinner and dine on our outdoor patio or in our open breeze dining room.
